Privacy Policy for Ecrah

Ecrah does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. The app operates entirely offline and does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to use its features.

Ecrah currently does not integrate third-party ads or analytics. If future versions include services (like Google Analytics or AdMob), this Privacy Policy will be updated, and you will be notified.

Children’s Privacy

Ecrah is not directed toward children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted within the app and on our public Privacy Policy page.
